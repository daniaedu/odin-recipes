Arugula Salad

A vibrant arugula salad that's as nutritious as it is delicious. Fresh arugula serves as the base for a medley of colorful cherry tomatoes, crisp cucumber and tangy red onion. Topped with crumbled feta, crunchy nuts, and the pop of pomegranate seeds, this salad is a true celebration of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • Fresh arugula
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Pomegranate seeds
  • Walnuts, pecans or almonds, chopped
  • Balsalmic vinagrette dressing

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the arugula thoroughly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the arugula, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, and red onion.
  3. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese, pomegranate seeds, and chopped nuts over the salad.
  4. Drizzle balsamic vinaigrette dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve the arugula salad as a refreshing and nutrient-dense option for a light meal or side dish.
