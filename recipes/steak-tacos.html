Steak Tacos

Description

Savor the bold flavors of perfectly grilled steak in these mouthwatering tacos. Tender slices of steak, seasoned to perfection, are nestled in warm tortillas. Complete with vibrant bell peppers, onions, tomatoes, avocados and zesty cilantro, these tacos are a delightful explosion of taste in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Seasoned steak (flank, skirt, or your preferred cut)
  • Flour or corn tortillas
  • Sliced onions
  • Sliced bell peppers
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Chopped fresh cilantro
  • Sliced avocado
  • Lime wedges
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings like shredded cheese, hot sauce, etc

Steps

  1. Rub the steak with your preferred seasoning
  2. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the steak to your preferred doneness.
  3. Allow the steak to rest for a few minutes before slicing it thinly against the grain.
  4. Warm the tortillas on the grill or in a dry skillet.
  5. Fill each tortilla with slices of steak, onions, tomatoes, bell peppers, chopped cilantro and avocados
  6. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the tacos.
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste
  8. Add optional toppings like shredded cheese, hot sauce, etc
  9. Serve the steak tacos hot and enjoy!
