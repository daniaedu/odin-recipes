Caprese Salad

Description

A delightful Caprese salad with a twist! Juicy tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and aromatic basil leaves are drizzled with extra-virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Elevate the flavors with a dash of red pepper flakes for a subtle, spicy kick.

Ingredients

  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Balsalmic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • red pepper flakes (for spiciness)

Steps

  1. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella cheese into evenly thick slices.
  2. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate, alternating them and slightly overlapping.
  3. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the slices.
  4. Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the salad.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for some heat.
  6. Serve immediately as a refreshing appetizer or side dish.
